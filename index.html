What is SheetPilot?

SheetPilot is a Christ-centered business providing done-for-you spreadsheet automation systems for entrepreneurs, ministries, nonprofits, and small businesses. Whether you’re managing data, building dashboards, or streamlining workflows with Google Drive, Gmail, and Microsoft Excel, SheetPilot simplifies the process — so you don’t have to.

Led by the Holy Spirit of God, we approach every project with excellence, integrity, and a heart to serve.

What We Do

  • Custom spreadsheet automation for Google Sheets or Microsoft Excel
  • Email notifications, triggers, and scheduled tasks
  • Drive file management & form integrations
  • Data cleanup and sorting logic
  • Dashboards and multi-tab systems
  • Training, walkthroughs, and documentation
